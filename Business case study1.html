Aqua City Water Management Model

Introduction

Aqua City is a rapidly growing urban area that has faced challenges with water management due to its increasing population and limited natural water resources. To address this, the city has implemented a sustainable and smart water management system.

Smart Water Supply System

The city has developed a Smart Water Grid that efficiently monitors and distributes water based on real-time demand and consumption patterns. Key features include:

  • IoT-based water meters for accurate consumption tracking.
  • Automated leak detection to minimize wastage.
  • AI algorithms for predictive maintenance of pipelines.

Rainwater Harvesting and Reuse

Aqua City has mandated the installation of rainwater harvesting systems in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Collected rainwater is filtered and reused for non-potable purposes, such as:

  • Landscape irrigation.
  • Cleaning and cooling systems.
  • Flushing in households.

Wastewater Treatment and Recycling

The city operates advanced wastewater treatment plants that convert sewage into safe, reusable water for industrial and agricultural use. The government has also invested in:

  • Community-level treatment units.
  • Incentives for industries adopting recycled water.
